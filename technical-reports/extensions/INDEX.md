## How to Add Your Extension

1. Copy the template: `cp EXTENSION_TEMPLATE.md my_extension_name.md`
2. Fill in your research design
3. Create a directory: `mkdir -p my_extension_name/{data,scripts,manuscript}`
4. Open a Pull Request or submit to lila.lab0x@gmail.com
