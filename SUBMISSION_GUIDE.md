## Reviewer Addressing Strategy

### Common Critique 1: "Why focus on Bangla if you review global literature?"
**Response**: Bangla (265M speakers, 7th largest) exemplifies geographic gap. 
Literature shows zero economic sentiment indices for Bangla despite 
similar work for smaller-speaker-count languages. BENI demonstrates how 
systematic review findings apply to underrepresented regions.

### Common Critique 2: "Sentiment analysis already well-studied; what's novel?"
**Response**: This is \textit{first systematic review} quantifying 
forecast improvements across domains, documenting geographic/linguistic gaps, 
and explicitly proposing expansion agenda with case study. Prior literature 
fragmented across journals; we consolidate and identify structural absences.

### Common Critique 3: "LLMs section feels rushed/incomplete."
**Response**: Acknowledge: LLM application to economics nascent (Kwon et al. 2025, 
Allard et al. 2024 only recent examples). Section intentionally forward-looking 
to position this review as timely. Future updates will track LLM maturation.
