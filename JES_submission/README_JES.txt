QUICK START: 4 STEPS TO SUBMISSION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: FINALIZE AUTHOR INFO (5 minutes)
  1. Open Title_Page_JES.txt
  2. Fill in author names, affiliations, corresponding author email
  3. Update Cover_Letter_JES.txt with author info and date
  4. Save updated files

STEP 2: VERIFY MANUSCRIPT (15 minutes)
  1. Open Manuscript_JES.pdf and verify:
     - No author names anywhere
     - All figures render correctly (Figure 1-3)
     - Tables are clear and readable
     - References formatted correctly
  2. Check word count: ~9,000 words
  3. Check page count: 31 pages

STEP 3: PREPARE SCHOLARONE SUBMISSION (10 minutes)
  1. Create account at: https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/jes
  2. Have ready:
     - Manuscript_JES.pdf
     - Title_Page_JES.txt (paste author info into ScholarOne)
     - Cover_Letter_JES.txt
  3. Prepare abstract, keywords, JEL codes for copy-paste

STEP 4: SUBMIT (20 minutes)
  1. Log into ScholarOne
  2. Select "Survey Article" as article type
  3. Enter metadata (title, abstract, keywords, JEL codes)
  4. Upload Manuscript_JES.pdf
  5. Upload Title_Page_JES.txt (as separate file)
  6. Upload Cover_Letter_JES.txt
  7. Suggest 5-6 reviewers (optional but recommended)
  8. Preview and submit
  9. Save confirmation email and manuscript ID

TOTAL TIME: ~50 minutes
