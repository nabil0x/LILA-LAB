QUICK START: SUBMISSION IN 3 STEPS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Verify Manuscript (5 minutes)
  □ Open Manuscript_IJF.pdf
  □ Check: 26 pages, all figures visible, readable
  □ Verify: No author names in text (blind review)

STEP 2: Prepare Submission (10 minutes)
  □ Read Title_Page_IJF.txt
  □ Fill in YOUR author information in blank fields
  □ Copy abstract from Title_Page_IJF.txt
  □ Copy keywords from Title_Page_IJF.txt

STEP 3: Submit (15 minutes)
  □ Go to: https://www.editorialmanager.com/ijf/
  □ Click "New Submission"
  □ Upload Manuscript_IJF.pdf
  □ Paste abstract, keywords, JEL codes
  □ Upload Cover_Letter_IJF.txt
  □ Submit!
